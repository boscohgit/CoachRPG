## Strength Note

Dumbbell isolation work is currently limited to 3 kg because these movements rely on smaller muscles, longer lever arms and stricter stabilization. Machine loads like 30-50 kg are not directly comparable because pulleys, leverage, bilateral assistance and larger muscle groups change the effective load.
